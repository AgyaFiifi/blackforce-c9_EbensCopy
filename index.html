Projects

Salesforce-Based Patient Care Management System for HealthBridge

This project involves the design and implementation of a comprehensive Patient Care Management System for HealthBridge, a growing healthcare services provider, using Salesforce core platform features. The solution centralizes patient data, streamlines appointment scheduling, and enhances patient engagement through an integrated digital platform. The system aims to address critical operational challenges including fragmented data and inefficient scheduling.

Technologies used includes Salesforce Platform, Flow Automation, Custom Objects and Record Types, Reports and Dashboards, Validation Rules and Formula Fields, Field-Level Security and Encrypted Text Fields.

The platform enhances operational efficiency and patient outcomes by enabling real-time access to health data, promoting interdepartmental collaboration, and empowering patients through self-service tools. It positions HealthBridge to scale sustainably while maintaining high standards of care and data integrity

Conceptual Design of a Geothermal District Heating System Powered by a Wind Turbine for Butte Community in Montana, USA

This project presents a renewable energy-based district heating system for a proposed residential community in Butte, Montana. It integrates geothermal energy for thermal heat extraction and wind energy for electricity generation, ensuring a 100% off-grid, zero-carbon emission operation.

The project demonstrates the feasibility and efficiency of integrating geothermal and wind power in district heating applications.
